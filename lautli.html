Lautli

A facade listens.

The work Lautli was presented at the Lange Nacht der Museen 2025 in Graz. The accompanying facade projection was created in collaboration with Prof. Christina Poth, Tascha Schröer and Mila Weger.

Letterpress meets projection.
Dialect meets facade.

Lautli is an artistically research-based project on letterpress and dialect by Prof. Christina Poth at the Fachhochschule Potsdam. The Graz edition was developed in cooperation with DruckZeug in Graz, connecting traditional printmaking with contemporary projection techniques.

With thanks to Mila Weger for on-site organization and the entire DruckZeug team.
